The "TZK" is the MOD/MPMissions of CTI, which is developed basing on
CRCTI_1.0_@RES2C3C8-irParaZ The author optimized the design of CTI basing on his personal comprehension about CTI, added many practical functions and fixed discovered bugs. Since TZK_2.00, inspired by the "SE" mod of TZK, the author added some units and upgrades. And since TZK_2.12 the 2.01 Arma:Resistance is applied, which allows the author to design some MP-stable visual effects, edit strings, precisely check units ammunition, design radio channel as an enhancement of the action list, etc.

CTI is short of "Conquer the Island". WEST and EAST sides must make full use of their resources to build factories and buy battle objects, defeat enemy and win the game.
